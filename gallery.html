Carnival in Las Palmas is pure magic. The streets come alive with music, colors, and dancing. Everyone joins in, from locals to visitors. Costumes are outrageous and full of personality.

Carnival lasts for weeks, with parades, music, and street parties that turn the city into a nonstop celebration. It’s impossible not to smile and get swept up in the celebration!🥳

During Carnival in Las Palmas, the streets sometimes host “murga” performances - satirical musical groups that sing funny and clever songs about local life and politics! 🎭🎶
